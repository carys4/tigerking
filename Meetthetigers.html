Meet the Tigers


Enzo

This is Enzo, one of our male tigers. He is 10 years old and is one of our oldest tigers. He weighs 252kg and has a role as the leader of the pack. Enzo enjoys play fighting with his other friends of the group.

Lucy

This is Lucy. She is 8 yeras old and weighs 239 kg. Her favourite hobby is to sleep so she is very lazy unless there is food involved. She has a strong relationship with Enzo and they have had many cubs together.

Clay

This is Lucy and Enzo's cub, Clay. He is 6 weeks old and weighs 21 kg. He is sassy and likes to wind up the other, older tigers of the pack. We give the cubs many toys to play with at the zoo to provide enrichment and to help them improve their learning.
