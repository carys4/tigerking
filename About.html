A Little About The Park...

The park is situated on 16 acres and firstly began as a shelter for endangered and exotic species of animals. The park has been home to over 50 species of animals and 200 big cats! The Garold Wayne Exotic Animal Memorial Park was established in 1999 by Joe Exotic and dedicated to the memory of his brother Garold Wayne Schreibvogel. This year, Netflix released a documentary on the park. The series explores the deeply interconnected society of big cat conservationists and collectors in America, and the private zoos and sanctuaries set up for these amazing animals!

Speaking to Entertainment Weekly, Tiger King producers Eric Goode and Rebecca Chaiklin said the story of the Tiger King could be continued. Rebecca said: "We have a crazy amount of footage and it's a story that's still unfolding. We're not sure yet, but there could be a follow-up on this story because there's a lot that's still unfolding in it, and it'll be just as dramatic and just as colorful as what has unfolded these past few years."
